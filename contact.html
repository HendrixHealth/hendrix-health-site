What happens next

No high-pressure sales call.

One short conversation, a clear written recommendation, and a decision you can take back to your team.

  1. We reply within one working day.

    From your local client partner — usually faster.

  2. A 20-minute readiness conversation.

    We ask about your team, systems, consult types, and what success would look like for you.

  3. A short demo, if helpful.

    Heidi running on a real consult, in your specialty. Q&A with the people who'd actually use it.

  4. A written rollout recommendation.

    Plan, training mix, timeline, patient-comms pack, pricing pathway. Yours to review without us in the room.
