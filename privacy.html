1. What this policy covers

This policy applies to the website at hendrix.health and its sub-pages, including any enquiry form submissions, newsletter sign-ups, and email correspondence with our team. It is published by Hendrix Health Ltd ("Hendrix", "we", "us"), a company registered in Aotearoa New Zealand.

This policy does not cover personal or health information processed by the Heidi product itself during clinical use. Heidi is provided by Heidi Health Pty Ltd; how Heidi handles consultation data is described in Heidi's own privacy documentation and in our plain-language explainer at Safety & privacy.

2. Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to provide, plus a small amount of automatic technical data when you use the site.

TypeWhat it includesHow we collect it
Enquiry information Name, role, organisation, sector, email, phone (optional), interest selections, and the message you write You submit it via the contact form on hendrix.health/contact
Email correspondence Whatever you send us You email us directly at hello@hendrix.health or a named team address
Newsletter sign-up Email address (and any name you provide) You sign up to The Pulse via pulse.hendrix.health
Technical / log data IP address, browser, page path, referrer, timestamp Standard server logs, retained briefly for security and operational diagnostics
Anonymised analytics Aggregated page views and traffic sources Privacy-preserving analytics (no cross-site tracking, no advertising cookies)

3. Why we collect it

We collect personal information for the following purposes only:

  • To respond to your enquiry, schedule a call, or send the resources you've asked for
  • To deliver implementation, training, and ongoing support if you become a customer
  • To send you The Pulse if you've subscribed (unsubscribe at any time, link in every email)
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website and our internal systems
  • To meet our legal and contractual obligations

We do not use your personal information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties. We do not run programmatic advertising on this site.

4. Who we share it with

We share information only with parties that help us run our business, and only as much as they need to do their job.

  • Email and CRM providers — to receive, route, and follow up on enquiries.
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers — for website hosting, automation workflows, and email delivery.
  • Heidi Health Pty Ltd — where you specifically ask us to refer you, set up an account, or coordinate procurement on your behalf.
  • Professional advisers (legal, accounting, audit) — only where necessary and under confidentiality.
  • Government or regulators — only where we are legally required to.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Some of our service providers store data outside New Zealand (typically in Australia, the EU, or the United States). Where they do, we take reasonable steps to ensure comparable protection.

5. Cookies & analytics

hendrix.health uses a small number of strictly-necessary cookies (e.g. for session and security), and privacy-preserving aggregate analytics to understand how the site is used. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party trackers, or cross-site profiling.

You can block or delete cookies in your browser at any time without affecting your ability to read the site.

6. How long we keep it

  • Enquiry data — retained for as long as we have an active conversation with you, plus a reasonable period for follow-up. Typically 24 months unless you become a customer.
  • Customer records — retained for the life of the engagement and for the period required by NZ tax and contract law thereafter.
  • Newsletter list — retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Server logs — retained briefly (typically 30–90 days) for security and operational reasons, then deleted or aggregated.

You can ask us to delete your information sooner — see Your rights.

7. Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal information from loss, misuse, and unauthorised access. These include encryption in transit, access controls on internal systems, and routine review of our suppliers' security posture.

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever became aware of a privacy breach affecting your information, we would notify you and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner where required by law.

8. Your rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct information that is wrong or out of date
  • Withdraw consent for newsletter subscriptions or marketing emails
  • Ask us to delete your information, subject to any legal or contractual retention obligations
  • Complain to us, and onward to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner if you remain unsatisfied

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@hendrix.health. We will respond within twenty (20) working days.

9. Children

Our website and enquiry form are intended for healthcare professionals, organisations, and adults considering Hendrix's services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our site, please email us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of the page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be flagged on the website and, where relevant, communicated to active customers directly.

11. Contact & complaints

For any privacy question, request, or complaint, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@hendrix.health
  • General contact: hello@hendrix.health
  • Postal: Hendrix Health Ltd, Aotearoa New Zealand

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of New Zealand at privacy.org.nz.
