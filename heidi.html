Heidi Scribe · ambient documentation

The ambient scribe NZ clinicians actually keep using.

Listens during the consultation. Produces structured, EHR-ready notes that sound like the clinician. The clinician reviews, edits, and signs off. Works in noisy rooms, with multiple speakers, in 110+ languages including te reo Māori.

Ambient · No buttons mid-consult
  • 110+ languages with automatic detection, including te reo Māori
  • 200+ note templates across specialties; custom templates on paid plans
  • Sessions from 5 seconds to 2+ hours
  • Task extraction: referrals, tests and follow-ups compiled automatically
  • Works in noisy rooms and with multiple speakers
  • The clinician reviews and edits before anything enters the chart

Privacy, safety, and clinical responsibility

Patients consent. Audio is processed in real time and not retained as a recording. The clinician reviews and signs off every note. End-to-end encryption, least-privilege access, and de-identification before AI processing.

Heidi was reviewed and endorsed by Health New Zealand's National AI & Algorithm Expert Advisory Group (NAIAEAG) on 17 July 2025 following privacy, cybersecurity, and compliance review. Designed to support HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 27001, and SOC 2 requirements; full documentation pack available during procurement.
