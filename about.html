About Hendrix Health

Named after Leo Hendrix.
Built for the next generation of Aotearoa.

Hendrix Health is named after Leo Hendrix, Florian's son and Kalolo's godson. In ten years, when Leo is a teenager, we want him to grow up in a country with a more supportive, less-overworked healthcare system. That's the company we get up to build.

Founded June 2024 130 clinics visited 1,000+ clinicians met Twelve people today
Why we exist

Heidi builds the AI Care Partner. We make sure it lands well in Aotearoa.

Heidi makes a remarkable product. But software alone doesn't change clinical practice. What changes practice is the practice manager who's confident answering patient questions, the clinician who's been trained on a real consult in their own specialty, and the reception team who knows what to say when the phone rings.

That's the work Hendrix does. Sales, training, implementation and ongoing support. The part that turns a powerful AI Care Partner into something a clinic actually keeps using.

The founders

Florian and Kalolo started Hendrix in June 2024.

Before founding the company, they personally visited 130 NZ clinics and hospitals and met more than 1,000 clinical leaders. Hendrix was the answer to what they kept hearing.

FS
Florian Stroehle
Co-founder & CEO

Florian has spent 15+ years in digital healthcare across New Zealand and international markets, working on clinical systems, telehealth, scheduling, referrals, CRM and health-system transformation. At Hendrix he leads partnerships, strategy and enterprise implementation.

"We are on the same mission to support our clinical workforce who have been under pressure." — HINZ, March 2025

KH
Solosoloali'i Kalolo Haufano
Co-founder & COO

Kalolo has 10+ years of experience in adult learning, digital capability and workforce education, including Project Ikuna, the MAIA award-winning Māori and Pacific digital upskilling programme at Tātaki Auckland Unlimited. His background shapes Hendrix's practical training model, making complex AI tools understandable for real clinical teams.

What we believe

Our training programmes lift entire clinic teams, not just individual clinicians. Digital health literacy is part of the work, not a side effect of it.

Where we've been

Eighteen months. Founding to nationwide ED rollout.

The headline milestones. From a kitchen-table idea to having Heidi live in every NZ public emergency department, supported on the ground by Hendrix.

  1. June 2024
    Hendrix Health is founded

    After visiting 130 clinics and meeting 1,000+ clinicians, Florian and Kalolo formally launch Hendrix.

  2. 11 March 2025
    Heidi–Hendrix partnership announced

    HINZ announces the official Heidi Health × Hendrix Health partnership; Pulse+IT covers it as "Te Tiriti-friendly AI."

  3. 17 July 2025
    NAIAEAG endorsement

    Health NZ's National AI & Algorithm Expert Advisory Group endorses Heidi after privacy, cybersecurity, and compliance review.

  4. January 2026
    Every NZ public ED is on Heidi

    All 38 NZ public EDs are deployed: 1,000 ED + 100 mental-health crisis-team licences live. From pilot to nationwide in twelve months.
